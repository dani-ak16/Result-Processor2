File Format Requirements:

Your CSV/Excel file should have these columns:

  • full_name - Student's full name
  • ca1_score - First CA score (out of 5)
  • ca2_score - Second CA score (out of 5)
  • ca3_score - Third CA score (out of 5)
  • ca4_score - Fourth CA score (out of 5)
  • exam_score - Exam score (out of 80)

Note: Total score = CA1-4 (20 marks) + Exam (80 marks) = 100 marks total
